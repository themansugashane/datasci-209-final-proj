Life Expectancy Comparisons Over Time

This visualization is designed to help policymakers assess their country's progress in life expectancy over the past two decades. It brings together life expectancy and key related factors such as education spending, healthcare expenditure, healthcare coverage, and CO2 emissions .

By examining how these metrics evolve over time, policymakers can begin to evaluate the apparent impact of past policies in areas like healthcare and education funding or environmental regulation, and identify periods where changes in policy may align with improvements or stagnation in population health.

How to use this view:
Use the controls in the embedded dashboard to select a country and explore how life expectancy and related factors change over time beyond GDP alone.
